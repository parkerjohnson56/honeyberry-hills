Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rodriguez family, Honeyberry Hill has grown from a small 2-acre dream into Los Angeles' most beloved strawberry farm. Today, our 50-acre farm continues to be family-operated, with three generations working together to bring you the sweetest, most flavorful strawberries in Southern California.

Every berry carries the love, care, and dedication of our family. We believe the best strawberries come from happy plants, nurtured soil, and farmers who truly care about what they grow.

Eco-Conscious

100% organic farming with zero pesticides, using natural compost and beneficial insects to protect our crops

Family Legacy

Three generations of strawberry expertise, from grandparents who started the farm to grandchildren who help pick today

Farm Fresh

Hand-picked daily at peak ripeness, ensuring every strawberry reaches you at the perfect moment of sweetness

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, sunscreen, and a reusable water bottle. We provide containers for picking.

How long does picking take?

Most visitors spend 1-2 hours picking strawberries and exploring the farm.

What are your prices?

Strawberries are sold by weight. Current pricing is $5 per pound.
